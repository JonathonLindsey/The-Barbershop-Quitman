About Us

More Than Just A Haircut. Learn More About Us!

After 28 years living in Southeast Alaska my wife and I moved to Quitman to be close to my father-in-law. He lives in Heber Springs and he always said we'd eventually move here. I worked for a hospital in Ketchikan as the Food & Environmental Services manager. It was a good job and I worked with some wonderful people. Becoming a barber was a dream of mine. So, when we decided to move I looked for Barber Colleges and found that there were several not too far from Quitman. I enrolled October of 2021 and began learning the craft. When I wasn't in class I was practicing at home on mannequin heads and watched videos. I drilled with shears, straight razor and clippers until they felt natural. September of 2022 my youngest son came down from Alaska to be my model for the state barber board test. I passed the test and became a licensed barber.

Since 2021

I have been putting a smile on clients face one haircut and beard trim at a time for the past 2 years. And will be for the expected future.

100+ clients

It has been a crazy journey and I have loved every second of it, and I wouldn't be where I am today without all my amazing clients.
